FAQ

What is "Background Image URL" ?

It's an image assigned to each individual assignment to give an aesthetic feel to the particular assignment and motivation to the students

How can I find "Background Image URL" ?

Step 1 : Go to unsplash.com

Step 2 : On the search bar, type the keyword related to the assignment.You'll be presented with lot of images.

Step 3 : Click on the image you like

Step 4 : Now right click on the image and click "open image in new tab

Step 4 : Image will be opened in new tab.Now copy the link in the URL bar

Step 4 : Paste that URL in the above box and you are good to go!
